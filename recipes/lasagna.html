Classic Lasagna

Main Page

Ingredients:

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage (optional, or use an additional pound of ground beef)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (28 ounces each) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (or 1 tablespoon dried)
  • 1 bay leaf

For the Ricotta Mixture:

  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (or 1 tablespoon dried)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For Assembling:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced or shredded
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, cook the ground beef, Italian sausage, and onion over medium heat until the meat is browned and the onion is tender. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  2. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, red wine (if using), sugar, basil, oregano, salt, black pepper, crushed red pepper flakes, fresh parsley, and bay leaf.
  3. Reduce the heat and let the sauce simmer for at least 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove the bay leaf before assembling the lasagna.

2. Cook the Lasagna Noodles:

  1. While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the lasagna noodles and cook for about 8-10 minutes, or until al dente. Drain the noodles and rinse them with cold water to prevent them from sticking together.

3. Prepare the Ricotta Mixture:

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, beaten egg, grated Parmesan cheese, fresh parsley, and salt. Mix well.

4. Assemble the Lasagna:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  3. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then top with 1/3 of the mozzarella slices or shredded mozzarella.
  4. Repeat the layers: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, two more times, ending with a final layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.

5. Bake the Lasagna:

  1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil, making sure to tent it slightly to prevent the cheese from sticking.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  3. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the lasagna is bubbly and the cheese is golden brown.

6. Serve:

  1. Allow the lasagna to rest for about 10-15 minutes before slicing. This helps it set and makes it easier to serve.
  2. Slice and serve hot, garnished with additional fresh parsley or basil if desired.

Enjoy!
